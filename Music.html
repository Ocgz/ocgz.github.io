New Single

Moth To A Flame

The third single from Swedish House Mafia's long-awaited album Paradise Again. The Weeknd collaborated with the electronic house dance trio to bring a catchy dance song with a groovy house production. The Weeknd brings smooth vocals to the song about a love triangle and raw emotions of pursuing a weak relation.
